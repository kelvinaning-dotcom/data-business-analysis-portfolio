# Business Analyst Portfolio

## Professional Summary
Business analyst experienced in gathering requirements, analyzing processes, and translating business needs into data-supported solutions. Proven ability to evaluate workflows, define performance metrics, and support stakeholders through documentation, analysis, and reporting.

## Core Business Analysis Competencies
- Requirements elicitation and documentation
- Stakeholder engagement and communication
- Current-state and future-state process mapping
- Gap and impact analysis
- KPI and performance metric definition
- User acceptance testing (UAT) support

## Business Analysis Methods
- Stakeholder interviews and requirement workshops
- Process flow and workflow analysis
- Functional and non-functional requirement documentation
- Risk and impact assessment
- Validation and verification support

## Tools and Techniques
Excel | Power BI | Process Mapping | Documentation Standards

## Representative Business Analysis Work

### Process Improvement and Workflow Analysis
**Objective:** Evaluate existing workflows to identify inefficiencies, risks, and improvement opportunities.

**Approach:**
- Documented current-state processes and decision points
- Identified bottlenecks, redundancies, and control gaps
- Developed future-state workflow recommendations

**Outcome:** Supported improved efficiency, consistency, and alignment with business and compliance requirements.

---

### Performance Metric Definition
**Objective:** Establish clear, consistent metrics to support performance monitoring and decision-making.

**Approach:**
- Collaborated with stakeholders to define KPIs
- Documented metric definitions and calculation logic
- Supported reporting and dashboard development

**Outcome:** Improved clarity and consistency in performance reporting across stakeholders.

## Deliverables and Outputs
- Business and functional requirements documents
- Process maps and workflow diagrams
- KPI frameworks and metric definitions
- Stakeholder presentations and briefings
- UAT support documentation

## Role Alignment
This portfolio aligns with Business Analyst roles focused on:
- Requirements analysis
- Process improvement
- Stakeholder support
- Performance and reporting alignment
